Merlin Labs, the Center of Innovation for HomeServices of America, was established in October 2017 in Austin, TX.

One of Merlin's overarching product goals is to create a consumer-led real estate platform that empowers more people to experience homeownership and connect them with the right experts (our agents and mortgage loan officers) to guide them through the process.

The two core offerings of the Merlin Platform are the Brokerage Website and the Content Management System.

The Merlin Platform is a responsive website that works great across desktop to mobile devices.
